Residential Products & Services



Series A Solar Panel - $300

Our very own manufactured monocrystalline solar panels that are the most efficient as well as the most commonly used residential solar panel solution.


The solar cells within monocrystalline panels are a single, flat black color, which makes them popular among homeowners. Monocrystalline panel efficiencies can range from 17% to 22%.



Series B Solar Panel - $150

Our very own manufactured polycrystalline solar panels that provide a cheaper solar panel option for your home.



These are popular among homeowners looking to install solar panels on a budget. Usually, polycrystalline silicon solar cells won’t have the corners cut off of them, so you won’t see the large white spaces on the front of the panel that you see on monocrystalline panels. Polycrystalline panel efficiency ratings will typically range from 15% to 17%.



1.0 Inverters - ($1,100/12 panels)

An EMAJJ developed and manufactured inverter solution for homeowners on a budget.



This inverter is necessary for every twelve solar panels on your roof, and serves as both the communication hub and energy transistor for your solar panels. The communication hub monitors the set of panels connected to each inverter to alert us if anything’s wrong. It also allows us to track the efficiency and production of your installation.



2.0 Microinverters - ($200/panel)

An EMAJJ developed and manufactured inverter solution for homeowners seeking maximum efficiency from their solar energy installation.



These inverters are specifically made for each individual solar panel on your roof, and all connect to the separate communication hub that monitors each individual set of inverters and panels to alert us if anything’s wrong. The hub also allows us to track the efficiency and production of your installation. The communication hub is covered by the upfront cost of microinverters.



Storage System - ($2,000) +

With EMAJJ’s storage system, when the grid is down, you have power. And when the grid is up, you can save money.



Through purchasing this optional add-on to the rest of our integrated solar package, you’ll be able to take advantage of 10.08 kWh and 3.84 kVA All-in-one Energy Storage. With the included smart switch, the EMAJJ storage system senses when it is optimal to charge or discharge the battery so that energy is stored when it is abundant and used when scarce. It is also capable of detecting when provision of backup power is needed.
